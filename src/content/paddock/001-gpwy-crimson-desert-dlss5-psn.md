Had a great time joining Dan, Matthew, and Matt on the [Good Playing With You](https://www.goodplayingwithyou.com) podcast this week. These guys run a weekly gaming news show and they don't pull punches. Real takes, no fluff.

## What We Covered

**Crimson Desert reviews are in** and they're all over the place. We dug into whether it's a must-buy, wait-for-sale, or skip entirely. The launch also tanked the parent company's stock, which led to some interesting conversation about how the market reacts to game reviews in real time.

**NVIDIA DLSS 5 is catching heat.** The AI upscaling conversation keeps getting more complicated. We talked through what DLSS 5 actually changes, why people are frustrated, and whether AI-generated frames are the future of graphics or a shortcut that's going to age poorly.

**Sony is retiring the PSN name.** This one hit close to home since I'd just published [my theory on what Sony is really building](/sprint/007-sony-playstation-ecosystem). We got into the why behind the rebrand and what it signals about PlayStation's future as a platform.

**Subnautica 2 release drama.** An update on the ongoing situation for anyone following that.

## What We've Been Playing

I talked about my [Slay the Spire 2](/sprint/005-slay-the-spire-2) addiction. Matt is deep into Marathon and loving it. The crew also had hands-on impressions of Toxic Commando.
